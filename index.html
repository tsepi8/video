Active Download Protection Measures

We've implemented multiple layers of security to prevent unauthorized downloading of this video:

Right-Click Disabled

Context menu is disabled on the video player to prevent "Save Video As" option.

Blob URL Protection

Video is served via Blob URLs, making it harder to find the direct source URL.

Download Attribute

The "download" attribute is removed from all anchor elements on the page.

DevTools Detection

Attempts to open browser DevTools are detected and video playback is paused.

Keyboard Shortcuts Blocked

Common screenshot and save shortcuts (Ctrl+S, Ctrl+Shift+S, etc.) are disabled.

Streaming Protection

Video is split into segments and streamed, preventing a single downloadable file.

Important Disclaimer

No client-side protection method is 100% effective. Determined users with technical knowledge may still find ways to capture or download video content. For maximum security, consider using DRM-protected streaming services or server-side protection measures.
